# VaakSetu Demo Script

## 5-Minute Presentation Script (for jury)

### Intro (30 seconds)
"Karnataka's 1092 helpline handles calls in Kannada, Hindi, English — often from citizens in crisis. Current agents struggle with dialect variations, miss emergencies, and can't verify understanding. VaakSetu is an AI Voice Bridge that solves all three."

### Scenario 1: The Language Bridge (90 seconds)
1. Click **Scenario 1** on dashboard
2. Show: Kannada transcript appearing with typing animation
3. Point out: Language auto-detected as Kannada
4. Show: AI Interpretation — intent (infrastructure/road), location (Rajajinagar)
5. Show: UTCS score is LOW (145) — green, normal processing
6. Show: Verification Loop — VaakSetu speaks back in Kannada
7. Click **Yes** — confirmed, summary stored

**Key message:** "VaakSetu understands dialect variations across Karnataka — North, Coastal, Old Mysore, Hyderabad-Karnataka."

### Scenario 2: Keyword Emergency (90 seconds)
1. Click **Scenario 2**
2. Watch: Dashboard EXPLODES red — UTCS jumps to 750
3. Show: 3 Tier-1 keywords highlighted: "bachao", "maar raha hai", "help karo"
4. Show: Emotion bars — Panic 92%, Fear 85%
5. Show: Alert banner flashing at top
6. Show: AI says "IMMEDIATE DISPATCH — Police + Ambulance"
7. Click **TAKE OVER CALL** — human takeover activated

**Key message:** "Tier-1 keywords trigger INSTANT alerts — before AI even finishes processing."

### Scenario 3: The Silent Emergency (90 seconds)
1. Click **Scenario 3**
2. Show: Transcript is just "...help..."
3. Show: But Stream C (Background Noise) lights up — Screaming 85%, Struggle 72%
4. Show: UTCS still hits 620 CRITICAL — from noise alone
5. Show: "This is the magic — even when a citizen CAN'T speak, VaakSetu hears the danger."

**Key message:** "Multi-signal detection means no emergency goes unnoticed."

### Closing (30 seconds)
"VaakSetu doesn't replace agents — it empowers them. Every interpretation is verified. Every correction feeds the learning pipeline. This is AI that understands before it acts, and asks before it assumes."
